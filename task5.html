angela's recipe for the best cinnamon rolls

ingredients

for the dough:

  • 3/4 cup waram milk
  • 21/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • egg plus 1egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

for the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

instructions

  1. mix the milk with the yeast ,sugar, eggs.
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture .
  3. add in the flour and mix untill combined into a dough
  4. knead the douht for 1o min
  5. transfer the dought into a large bowl 1 and cover with plastic warp. leave it some where to rise for 2 hours.
  6. after the dough has doubled in size roll it out into a large ractangle
